HeavenHelpers Orphanage

HeavenHelpers was established to address the growing need for safe housing and care for orphaned and vulnerable children in a remote village in Kisii County, Kenya. Over the years we have supported hundreds of children, many of whom have gone on to lead successful, fulfilling lives.

We provide a safe, loving, and nurturing environment — supporting each child's emotional, physical, and educational development so that every child has a genuine opportunity to thrive.

We envision a world where every child has a stable home, access to quality education, and the support to realise their full potential.

Compassion

Treating every child with kindness in a nurturing environment where they feel safe and valued.

Empowerment

Equipping children through education and life skills for a confident, independent future.

Community

Fostering belonging among children, staff, and supporters — a genuine network of care.

Integrity

Full transparency in how donations are received and used — accountable to every supporter.

Our children attend school daily, receive nutritious meals, access healthcare, and grow in a community that believes in their future. Every child has a name, a story, and unlimited potential.
