HeavenlyHelpers Orphanage

Heavenly Helpers Orphanage addresses the growing need for a housing and support for orphan kids, needy children, widows and elderly in the remote village in Kisii, Kenya. At Heavenly Helpers Orphanage, we are dedicated to provide a safe, lovely, loving and nurturing home for orphan kids and the vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide quality education, basic needs, medical services to ensure every child has the opportunity to thrive

We believe that with love, care and guidance, every child can build a brighter future.

We invite you to be part of our journey to create a loving home for children in need, whether through donations, volunteering or spreading the word of God, your support will make a great difference in their lives.

Compassion

Treating every child with kindness in a nurturing environment where they feel safe and valued.

Empowerment

Equipping children through education and life skills for a confident, independent future.

Community

Fostering belonging among children, staff, and supporters a genuine network of care.

Integrity

Full transparency in how donations are received and used accountable to every supporter.

Our children attend school daily, receive nutritious meals, access healthcare, and grow in a community that believes in their future. Every child has a name, a story, and unlimited potential.
